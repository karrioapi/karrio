## Set up carrier connections

To set up carrier connections, follow these steps:

1 - Log in to your Karrio account.

:::info
Once logged in, we will switch to test mode by clicking on the **View test data** menu to work with
karrio in test mode calling carrier sandbox APIs.
:::

<img style={{ borderRadius: 5 }} src="/img/assets/quick-start/test-mode.png" />

2 - Click on **Carriers** in the left navigation bar.

<img style={{ borderRadius: 5 }} src="/img/assets/quick-start/carriers.png" />

3 - Click **Register a carrier** to set up a carrier connection.

4 - Add the carrier account and credentials and click **Submit**.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/register-carrier.png"
/>

5 - Make sure the carrier connection is active.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/your-accounts.png"
/>

6 - You can also enable system carrier connections available to all Karrio users.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/system-accounts.png"
/>

## Generate a shipping label

Once you have set up carrier connections, you can now use those connections for shipping transactions.

To generate a shipping label, follow these steps:

1 - Click on **Shipping label** from the **Create** dropdown menu on the top bar.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/create-shipment-action.png"
/>

2 - Enter the Recipient and Shipper addresses to get live rates.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/fetch-rates.png"
/>

3 - Select your preferred shipping service rate and click **Buy shipping label**.

_You will get redirected to the shipment details page_

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/shipment-details.png"
/>

4 - Click **Print label** to download and print your shipping label.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/print-label.png"
/>

## Track a shipment

1 - Click on **Trackers** in the left navigation bar.

:::info
Notice that the shipment you created in the previous step has an associated tracker.
:::

<img style={{ borderRadius: 5 }} src="/img/assets/quick-start/trackers.png" />

2 - Click **Track a shipment** to create a new tracker for any valide shipment.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/create-a-tracker.png"
/>

3 - Enter a tracking number and select the corresponding carrier connection then click **Submit**.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: 5 }}
  src="/img/assets/quick-start/track-a-shipment.png"
/>
